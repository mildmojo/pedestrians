Pedestrian Mining Corps: Baby Earth Needs Asteroids

Baby Earth is small. Baby Earth needs asteroids to survive! The Pedestrian Mining Corps is ready to help! Deploy pedestrians, average schmoes, into the Milky Way to bring rocks home for harvest.

Created in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 32, themed "Unconventional Weapon". Join the discussion and PLEASE RATE at the submission page for Pedestrian Mining Corps. Or hit me up on Twitter: @mildmojo

How To Play

Tap the screen to fling a pedestrian at the asteroid field. If your timing's right, the pedestrian will smack into the asteroid and begin the WHOLLY SCIENTIFIC PROCESS of JUMPING UP AND DOWN to push the asteroid back to Baby Earth.
